Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2025

This policy explains how TubeGrab handles user data.

1. Data Collection

TubeGrab does not collect personal data or use cookies. No user accounts are required. However, server logs store basic information:

  • Timestamps of downloads
  • YouTube URLs submitted
  • Chosen format (mp3/mp4/HD)
  • Technical status or errors

2. Purpose of Logs

Logs are used for technical debugging, abuse prevention, and performance monitoring. No tracking or analytics tools are used.

3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. TubeGrab is ad-free and tracker-free.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Log files are stored locally in the app/Log/ directory and retained only as needed for technical maintenance.

5. Security

Logs are stored securely and are accessible only to the administrator. No personal identifiers are stored.

6. User Rights

If you believe a log entry may involve sensitive data, you may request its review or deletion by emailing us.

7. Updates

This policy may evolve. Any significant change will be noted in the CHANGELOG.md file of the project.
